AI is no longer a future promise. It is already reshaping how work gets done, how teams are organized, and how value is created. This keynote takes a step back from tools and buzzwords to look at the bigger picture.

Drawing from current industry discourse and real-world adoption patterns, the talk explores how AI is moving from copilots to autonomous systems, how organizations are restructuring around these capabilities, and what this means for people working in tech-driven roles. It connects today's realities with near-future trajectories, highlighting where productivity gains are real, where expectations are inflated, and where human judgment still matters most.

The goal is to offer orientation, not prescriptions: helping the audience understand what is changing, what is likely coming next, and how to stay relevant without chasing every trend.
