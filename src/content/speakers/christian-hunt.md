Christian Hunt is the founder of Human Risk Limited, a behavioural-science-led consulting and training firm specializing in risk, compliance, conduct and ethics, and the author of "Humanizing Rules". He was previously Head of Behavioural Science at UBS and COO of the UK Prudential Regulation Authority.
