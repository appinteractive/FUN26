Robert Gollwitzer is a German attorney and specialist lawyer for employment law (Fachanwalt für Arbeitsrecht) at Link Siry Rechtsanwälte in Nuremberg, advising mid-sized companies and employees on individual and collective employment law. He speaks publicly on false self-employment.
